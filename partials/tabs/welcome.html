Lambda Summer of Code, 2017



Lambda Summer of Code is a program focused on introducing students to open source software development. Students work on a 3 month open source programming project during their break from university.

As a part of Lambda Summer of Code, student participants are paired with a mentor from a relevant domain, gaining exposure to real-world software development and techniques. Students have the opportunity to spend the summer earning a stipend while working in areas related to their interests.
